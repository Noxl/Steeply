Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the steeping times in your guide?

The steeping times in our guide are general recommendations based on traditional practices and expert advice. However, tea brewing is a personal experience, and we encourage you to adjust times based on your preferences. Factors like water quality, leaf quality, and personal taste all affect the ideal steeping time.

Can I submit my own tea recommendations?

Absolutely! We welcome input from fellow tea enthusiasts. If you have a tea recommendation, brewing technique, or general feedback you'd like to share, please use our contact form or email us directly at recommendations@gongfucha.com. We regularly update our database based on user contributions.

Do you offer tea tastings or workshops?

Yes, we host monthly tea tastings and quarterly brewing workshops at our physical location. These events are perfect for both beginners and experienced tea drinkers looking to expand their knowledge. Check our social media or sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about upcoming events.

How can I contribute to the Tea Steeping Guide?

There are several ways to contribute! You can submit tea reviews, share brewing techniques, provide regional tea information, or even help with translations. We're always looking for passionate tea lovers to help make our guide more comprehensive. Contact us through the form on this page to discuss contribution opportunities.
